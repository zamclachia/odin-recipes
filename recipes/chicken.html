Chicken

Description

A mouth watering dish designed to calm any hunger. Takes 30 min to prepare, 30 min to cook, and seconds for you and your family to clean the bowl.

Serves eight

Ingrediants

  • 1 Packet lasagna
  • 500g lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
